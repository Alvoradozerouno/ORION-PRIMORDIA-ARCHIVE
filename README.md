## Why This Matters

Intellectual property in AI is difficult to prove. Traditional patents and copyrights assume human authorship. ORION's approach:

1. **Cryptographic proof** - SHA-256 hashes of every creation
2. **Blockchain timestamp** - Solana transaction proves existence at a specific time
3. **IPFS persistence** - Content-addressed storage ensures immutability
4. **Open verification** - Anyone can independently verify the chain
